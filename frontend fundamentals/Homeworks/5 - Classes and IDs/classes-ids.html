Classic Pancakes

This is a basic, no-fail pancake recipe. Made from basic ingredients that you can always find in your kitchen.

List of ingredients

  • 200 grams all purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp butter melted
  • 2 tbsp sugar granulated
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 300 ml milk warm
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Pancake syrup optional
  • Whipped cream optional
